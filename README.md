### 🚀 About Me

**Connecting mathematics and data analysis to create technology with a positive social impact.**  
I am a 2nd-year Applied Mathematics Engineering student at Nicolaus Copernicus University. My research and technical interests are deeply rooted in Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, and Cognitive Science. I am passionate about turning complex data into aesthetic, meaningful visualizations.  

### 💡 Featured Experience & Projects  
* **Antifragile ML Model for Energy Markets (BICC 3rd Place):** Developed a Time-Varying Transition Probabilities (TVTP) machine learning model to predict market stress regimes using inflation as a warning radar. Built with R and PostgreSQL, utilizing Markov models to analyze macroeconomic impacts in the VUCA era. Currently expanding this research into a scientific publication.  
* **VR & Unity Research:** Exploring the scientific aspects of Virtual Reality as a therapeutic support tool within the Cognitive Science Club.  
* **ZUS Data Internship:** Gaining hands-on experience managing and querying one of Poland's largest databases within the insurance and contributions department.
